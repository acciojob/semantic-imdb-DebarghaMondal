Inception

Actors Quotes

Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction thriller film written and directed by Christopher Nolan. It stars Leonardo DiCaprio as a professional thief who steals information by infiltrating the subconscious of his targets. His final mission requires him to implant an idea into a CEO's subconscious mind.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio stars as Dom Cobb, a skilled thief specialized in the art of extraction.
